[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
The neon signs are fading, but your eyes are shining bright
I’ve been riding through the shadows just to find you here tonight
Pour a little whiskey, let the world just fade away
You’re the only destination where my restless body will stay

[Verse 1]
There’s a quiet in the tavern when you walk across the floor
Every mile I put behind me led me right up to your door
I can feel the tension rising when you press up close to me
You’re the sweet and tender midnight that I never want to leave

[Pre-Chorus]
I can hear the music slowing down to just a steady beat
There’s a dangerous attraction pulling me out of my seat
Let me pull you against my body, feel the heat of your embrace
We’ve got nothing but the moment and a slow and sensual pace

[Chorus]
I’m your last call cowboy, reaching for your hand
In this cozy little corner where our wild hearts understand
Let me whisper my desire, let the candle burn on down
We can make our own forever in the silence of this town

[Post-Chorus]
Just you and me, falling deep into the night
Hold me close, let me love you till the morning light

[Verse 2]
I can read the hidden stories in the corners of your smile
Let me take you on a journey, let me stay a little while
There’s a rhythm in the way you breathe that matches up with mine
Every time I trace your skin, I taste the sweetness of the wine
